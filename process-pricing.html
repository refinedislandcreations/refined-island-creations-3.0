Our Process

1.

Schedule a call with us!

During this consultation, we will explore your needs and goals, creating a scope of work for your custom website. This is crucial for your luxury property's essence to be accurately reflected. Let us start building your digital sanctuary today.

Book A Call Today

2.

Wireframe & Moodboard

Next, we will create a mood board, sitemap, and wireframe based on your vision. This blueprint will give you a preview of the design's look and feel, aligning with your brand identity and goals.

3.

Website Design - Bringing Your Vision to Life

We will create a customized website that reflects your brand and meets your goals. With placeholder content and images, you will see your website's captivating essence come to life.

4.

Coding Your Website - Making It Real

Once the design is approved, we will use HTML, CSS, jQuery & Jekyll to build your website for a seamless digital experience. You can review your website on a temporary domain for feedback.

5.

Testing & Connect Live Domain - Your Website Goes Live

Before the grand reveal, we will test your website's performance on various devices and browsers to ensure fast loading and impressive appearance. Once we have made the necessary tweaks, we will connect your domain, and your personalized website will be ready for the world to admire.
