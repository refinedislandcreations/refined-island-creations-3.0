Process and Timeline

How It Works:

  1. Consultation Call: We begin with a deep-dive consultation to fully understand your brand’s needs and create a clear scope of work.
  2. Mood Board & Wireframe Creation: You’ll be able to collaborate on a detailed blueprint of your site’s structure, design, and functionality.
  3. Custom Design & Development: Once the wireframe is approved, we proceed to design and develop your fully custom website, built to your exact specifications.
  4. Final Touches: After testing, we’ll connect your domain and ensure everything is ready for launch.

Timeline:

Due to the bespoke nature of this package, the timeline can vary from 4 to 8 weeks depending on your requirements and content availability. High-quality images and copywriting will significantly impact the project’s pace. Copywriting assistance is available upon request but may affect the final price.

Price and Additional Costs

Price: starts at $2,500. With final pricing based on the number of pages and the complexity of the design.

Additional Costs:

Please note that the domain name, hosting, and CMS are not included in our package price. These services will need to be purchased separately.

  • Domain: If you already have a domain name, we can link it to your website. If not, we recommend purchasing your domain through GoDaddy or Google Domains, typically costing between $10 to $20 per year.
  • Netlify: Your external hosting provider. Most clients find the free plan sufficient, but the pro plan is approximately $19 per month.
  • CloudCannon: The content management system (CMS) that allows easy website editing. Using our partner link, CloudCannon costs only $10 per month.
